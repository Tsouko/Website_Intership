BEST STATISTICS PAPER AWARD


Best Statistics Paper Award for Young Statisticians

The award was proposed in 2005 (Panhellenic Statistics Conference in Rhodes) by Prof. N. Balakrishnan (an Honorary Μember of GSI since
technology developments in analytical reasoning, interaction, data transformations and representations for computation and visualization,
2007). 12 young statisticians are the recipients of the award and 5 praises have been given during the period 2006-2020.

A young statistician is considered to be a master student, a PhD candidate or holder (with at most 3 years since the PhD was awarded). Τhe
term “young” refers to new researchers in Probability and Statistics and it does not in any way refer to the candidate’s age.

As of this year the award is upgraded, with the support of Professor Balakrishnan who is financing the award which will be accompanied
annually, by the amount of 200 euros and by waiving the annual membership fee for a 2-year period. The competition will take place on
Sunday September 26, 2021 during the 2021 (Virtual) International Workshop of the Conference.

All those interested are kindly requested to express by email (secretariat@esi-stat.gr) (a) their intention to participate in the special session
for the award by June 10, 2021 and then

(b) arrange to submit the full paper to the same electronic address, by July 10, 2021.
