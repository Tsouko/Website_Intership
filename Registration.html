REGISTRATION


For attending the Conference, you are kindly requested to proceed with the payment of the registration fee according to the information (table) provided below.


Participants without presentation are asked to fill in and submit the Registration Form. In case of filling the form after September 6, 2021,
please add 20€ to the registration fee.

Participants with presentation are asked to prepare the abstract of the presentation according to the guidelines in Call & Submission and fill in and submit the Registration Form by July 25, 2021. Registrations after the deadline of 25/7/21 will be considered as long as the planning of the scientific program of the Conference allows it.
